Introduction
 - This is a game developed by a student of "University of Applied Sciences Europe".
 - The game is made by Geumseong Han.
 - The aim of the game is to hit the target as many times as possible by launching the ball, without missing the target.
 - Run "GSH_Game_1.exe" to play the game.

How to play
 - Launch balls with "Spacebar".
 - Hold "Spacebar" to launch further.
